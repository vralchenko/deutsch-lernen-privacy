Data Deletion Instructions

App: Deutsch Lernen

Developer: Viktor Ralchenko

How to delete your data

Deutsch Lernen stores all user data locally on your device. No data is stored on our servers. You can delete all your data using either method below:

Method 1: Clear App Data

  1. Open Settings on your Android device
  2. Go to Apps (or Applications)
  3. Find and tap Deutsch Lernen
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data (or Clear Storage)

This will delete all your learning progress, flashcard history, chat messages, and preferences.

Method 2: Uninstall the App

  1. Long-press the Deutsch Lernen icon on your home screen
  2. Tap Uninstall

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data permanently.

What data is deleted

  • Vocabulary review history and flashcard ratings
  • Grammar exercise scores
  • Chat conversation history
  • Streaks and study statistics
  • User preferences (level, daily goal, TTS speed, etc.)
Note: If you have an active Premium subscription, deleting app data does not cancel your subscription. To cancel your subscription, go to Google Play Store > Subscriptions > Deutsch Lernen > Cancel.
